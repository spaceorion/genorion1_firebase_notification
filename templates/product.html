Our Product


Compact, Secure & Compatible

Gen-M12
GEN-M12 is the main module which is required as only 1 device per space. Works in conjunction with a number of GEN-S12 devices, creating a mesh to connect the whole home/office space as a centralized system. The device integrates a series of smart features & multiple sensors.

Features

  Only 1 device is required.

  Can work standalone or as Main module.

  Supports calling feature (With sim).

  Control up to 12 devices.

  3 speed control switches.
  8 loads upto 10A and 1 heavy load upto 15A.
  Access sharing with admin roles.
  Lifetime free Software upgrades for device.
  Continuous Notification via app, calls and SMS.

  Fire, gas, light, temperature & humidity sensing.

  IR support for controlling remote based devices.
  Battery backup of 6-12 hours.

  Power consumption analysis.
  Easy upgrades with plug & play devices.
  Can be controlled via SMS & Bluetooth.

Gen-S12
GEN-S12 is the sub module, which can be installed as many as required in a home, office or building. It works perfectly with the GEN-M and extends the capabilities of all the desired functionalities of GEN-M series. It creates a smart mesh with other GEN-S devices and Gem-M for continuous connectivity without internet. The device smartly integrates a series of smart features & multiple sensors.

Features

  Can work standalone or as Sub module.

  Continuous Notification via app.

  Control up to 12 devices.

  3 speed control switches.

  Fire, gas, light, temperature & humidity sensing.
  Power consumption analysis.
  Battery backup of 6-12 hours.

  Easy upgrades with plug & play devices.

  Access sharing with admin roles.
  Lifetime free Software upgrades for device.
